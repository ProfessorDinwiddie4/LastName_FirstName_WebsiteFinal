Who We Are

Kasama is a bakery and modern Filipino restaurant from chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores located in the East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago. Kasama is unique with a 13-course dinner tasting menu that emphatically displays this talented teams training, experience and heritage for an experience that is ambitious, clever, and distinct. The chefs have taken traditional Filipino cooking and given it a creative, contemporary spin.

We look forward to welcoming you!

What We Offer

Enjoy our wide selection of laminated pastries, as well as our sweet and savory apple and cheese croissants, chocolate chip cookies, huckleberry Basque cakes, and many more! Pair your pastry with one of our many flavored coffees, teas, or morning wines.

Take a bite out of our casual, summery bites like barbecue pork skewers (grilled pork shoulder, herb salad), charred corn (scallion mayonnaise, wasabi tobiko, nori citrus powder), and a Filipino breakfast with fried egg, garlic fried rice, and pickled green papaya.

Join us at the only Filipino tasting menu restaurant in Chicago and among a select few in America. Our dinner includes dishes like kare-kare, pancit and lumpia with ingredients like foie gras and wagyu. Our Modern Filipino tasting menu is reservation only Thursday - Sunday evening.
